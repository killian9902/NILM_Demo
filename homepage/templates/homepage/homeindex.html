About

This AI model is designed for Non-Intrusive Load Monitoring, also
known as Energy Disaggregation.

NILM is a transformative approach to understanding and optimising energy usage within residential and commerical spaces. By intelligently analysing electrical load data, NILM technology identifies and tracks the energy consumption of indidvudal appliances without needing direct sensor connections, paving the way for enhanced energy efficiency.

In our journey towards carbon neutrality, the pivotal challenge for energy providers lies in the transition to entirely clean energy production. In the interim, we can minimise energy wastage through NILM. By leveraging this technology, we empower consumers to identify and reduce unnecessary energy consumption, leading to a decrease in energy demand. This reduction alleviates the strain on our energy grids and also diminishes the carbon footprint associated with traditional energy production methods.
